How it works

Onsite training, start to finish

From first enquiry to certification, here's what to expect when we deliver the APLH course at your premises.

1

Get in touch

Tell us your group size, preferred dates, and venue location so we can confirm availability.

2

We travel to you

Our trainer arrives at your venue with everything needed to deliver the full-day course.

3

Sit the exam on the day

Candidates complete the 40-question exam under supervision, straight after the training.

4

Certification follows

Papers are verified and certificates issued through the usual awarding body process.

What you'll need to provide

Onsite delivery is straightforward, but there are a few things to check before booking.

See all delivery options →
  • A suitable room with enough space to sit the exam individually, without candidates seeing each other's papers
  • Up to 15 candidates per session — smaller or larger groups can be discussed
  • Valid photo ID for every candidate — passport or photocard driving licence
  • Timings can be arranged to suit your team, including evenings or weekends by arrangement
